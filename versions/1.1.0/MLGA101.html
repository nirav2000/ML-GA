What you are seeing (quick guide)

  • Target: the green circle. Dots are trying to evolve a movement sequence that reaches it.
  • Active dots: white dots still moving this generation. Yellow reached target. Red crashed.
  • Why it “stops suddenly”: each generation has a fixed number of moves (DNA length). At the end, simulation pauses briefly to score fitness, breed, and restart at step 0.
  • How they get better: fitter dots are picked more often as parents, their DNA is crossed over, and mutations create new variations.
  • Best trail: cyan path from the highest-fitness dot ever seen so far (across all generations).
